Menu

The menu at Ritual Salad is a little different than what you’re used to. You imagine it, we build it. Is it a bowl? Is it a salad? You decide. Create your own spellbinding salad by choosing your protein, veggies, some fun toppings to spice it up and then dressings of your dreams!! Finally pick one of our decadent sides. Creating a salad this good should be a Ritual.

Choose your base:

Spring Mix, Rice or Half and Half (mix of both)

Choose 1 protein:

Chicken, hard boiled eggs, cottage cheese, edamame, wild rice

Choose 3 veggies:

Marinated: Olives, jalapenos, mushrooms, broccoli, beets and artichoke hearts.

Roasted: Carrots, beets, brussels sprouts and sweet potatoes.

Fresh/Raw: Green onions, red onions, cherry tomatoes, stringed cucumbers, bell peppers, shredded carrots, corn, chickpeas, seasonal fruits.

Choose 3 toppings:

Bacon bits, feta cheese, blue cheese, shredded cheeses, pepitas/pumpkin seeds, sunflower seeds, chia/flax/hemp trio, dried fruits, GF* croutons, corn nuts, corn chips, honey sesame crisps.

Choose 2 dressings:

Magickal Maple Balsamic, Spellbound Sesame Ginger, Ostara Orange and Roasted Garlic, Greek Goddess Vinaigrette, Brujas Smoky Cilantro Lime, Beltane Blue Cheese, EBC Ranch*.

Choose 1 side:

NANA Bread, assorted local breads based on availability, Pita bread, GF* bread, GF* pita, GF* tortillas.

Add On’s and Extra’s:

Add on any extras you would like!! Avocado, dressings, proteins, cheeses, breads, chips
