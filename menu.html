Menu

The menu at Ritual Salad is a little different than what you’re used to. You imagine it, we build it. Is it a bowl? Is it a salad? You decide. Create your own spellbinding salad by choosing your protein, veggies, some fun toppings to spice it up and then dressings of your dreams!! Finally pick one of our decadent sides. Creating a salad this good should be a Ritual.

All salad and bowl creations are $18 + tax & a 2% credit card fee.

1. Start by picking your base:

Mixed greens, Rice or Half and Half (mix of both).

2. Pick 1 from the Proteins:

Chicken, 2 hard boiled eggs, cottage cheese, wild rice, edamame.

3. Pick 3 of your fav veggies:

Marinated: Olives, jalapenos, mushrooms, broccoli, pickled beets, artichoke hearts.

Roasted: Carrots, beets, brussels sprouts and sweet potatoes.

Fresh/Raw: Green onions, red onions, cherry tomatoes, stringed cucumbers, bell peppers, shredded carrots, corn, chickpeas, seasonal fruits.

4. Make it magical with 2 toppings:


*only one cheese please*

Bacon bits, feta cheese, blue cheese crumbles, shredded cheese (R), pepita, sunflower seeds, chia/flax/hemp trio, dried cranberries, croutons (GF), corn chips, honey sesame crisps.

5. Choose 2 house made dressings:


(Two 2 oz cups w each salad)

Magickal Maple Balsamic (V), Spellbound Sesame Ginger, Ostara Orange and Roasted Garlic (V), Brujas Smoky Cilantro Lime (V), Beltane Blue Cheese, Gaia's Green Goddess, Hecate's Mediterranean Vinaigrette, *EBC Ranch, Vegan Ranch.

6. And a side to finish it off:

Ba-NANA Bread, POSITIVELY Artisanal Bread (R), Pita, GF Tortillas, GF Bread.
